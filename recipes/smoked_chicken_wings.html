Smoked Chiken Wings

Description

Smoked chicken wings are always a treat. They can be the perfect snack for gameday or a pool side treat. You can also use different seasonings and sauces to really add variety. This recipe will just cover the foundation and you can choose the flavor you want to move toward. I typically add in drumsticks with the wings and use multiple different seasoning/sauce combos to add variety.

Prep Time Cook Time Total Time
30 mins 1 hours 1.5 hours

Ingredients

  • 1-large bag of wings (or drumsticks)
  • Sauce of Choice (here are a few examples)
    • Buffalo
    • Garlic
    • Habanero
    • BBQ
  • Seasoning of Choice (here are a few examples)
    • Lemon Pepper
    • Cavender's Seasoning Blend
    • Nashville Hot
    • Garlic Powder
  • Supplies
    • Smoker
    • Disposable Foil Pans
    • Large Tongs for flipping
    • Meat Thermometer

Prep

  1. Thaw chicken out fully, but keep cold.
  2. Liberally apply the seasoning of choice.
  3. Fire up your smoker to 350F and let it stabilize.

Cook

  1. Spread the pieces out on the smoker grate.
  2. Flip every few minutes to keep from burning one side.
  3. Cook until internal reaches 165F.
  4. Remove from smoker and place in foil pans.
  5. Add your sauce of choice and toss the pieces in it.
  6. Enjoy!
