Salt Crusted Potatoes

Description

These delicous potatoes are are a great side for any typ of beef, pork or chicken dish. It is incredibly easy to make these little salty golden potatoes. The melted butter and salty tast pair great with each other.

Prep Time Cook Time Total Time
5 mins 20 mins 25 mins

Ingredients

  • Golden Yukon Potatoes
  • 1 Stick Butter
  • 1/2 Cup Table Salt
  • Chopped Parsley

Supplies

  • Large Pot
  • Dish to Melt Butter
  • Large Slotted Spoon

Prep

  1. Fill Large pot 1/2 with water.
  2. Add salt and potatoes.

Cook

  1. Bring water to boil
  2. Let Boil for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Melt butter while potatoes are cooking.
  4. Once potatoes are done, remove from water with slotted spoon.
  5. Pour over melted butter and add chopped parsley to garnish.
  6. Enjoy!
