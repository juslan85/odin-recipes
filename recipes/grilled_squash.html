Grilled Squash and Zuchinni

Description

Grilled squash and zuchinni are great with many other grilled or smoked dishes. They pair well with all types of food and can be flavored in many ways with different seasonings. This is one of my go-to's for a great side dish.

Prep Time Cook Time Total Time
20 mins 20 mins 40 mins

Ingredients

  • 4-5 Medium Yellow Squash
  • 3-4 Medium Zuchinni
  • Seasoning of choice (I prefer a Cajun style)
    • Tony Chachere's Original Creole
    • Kinder's "The Blend"
    • Cavender's
  • Olive Oil
  • Supplies
    • Gallon Zipper Bag
    • Large Tongs

Prep

  1. Wash and cut into strips.
  2. Place strips in zipper bag with Olive Oil and Seasoning.
  3. Shake Bag to evenly distribute the seasoning.

Cook

  1. Pre heat grill to 350 F.
  2. Place on grill being careful not to let the strips fall through the grate.
  3. Grill until soft flipping every 5 minutes.
  4. Enjoy!
